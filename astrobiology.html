Astrobiology

Explore the possibility of life beyond Earth.

What is Astrobiology?

Astrobiology is the scientific study of life in the universe. It examines the conditions for life to arise, evolve, and survive, as well as the potential existence of life beyond Earth.

Key Questions in Astrobiology

  • Origins of Life: How did life begin on Earth?
  • Extraterrestrial Life: Is there life elsewhere in the universe?
  • Habitable Environments: What are the conditions required for life?

Astrobiology and Technology

  • Advanced telescopes for detecting exoplanets.
  • Space probes exploring potentially habitable environments.
  • Laboratory simulations of extraterrestrial conditions.

Why Study Astrobiology?

Astrobiology helps us understand the origins and future of life on Earth and provides insights into our place in the universe.
