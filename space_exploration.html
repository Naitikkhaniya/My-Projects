Space Exploration

Discover humanity's journey into the cosmos.

What is Space Exploration?

Space exploration is the ongoing discovery and exploration of celestial structures in outer space. It has been driven by both human curiosity and technological advancement.

Milestones in Space Exploration

  • 1957: Launch of Sputnik 1, the first artificial satellite.
  • 1969: Apollo 11 lands the first humans on the Moon.
  • 1998: Launch of the International Space Station (ISS).
  • 2021: Perseverance rover explores Mars.

Key Organizations

  • NASA: The United States' National Aeronautics and Space Administration.
  • ESA: The European Space Agency.
  • SpaceX: A private company revolutionizing space travel.
  • Roscosmos: Russia's state space corporation.

Future of Space Exploration

Future endeavors include manned missions to Mars, the establishment of lunar bases, and the exploration of distant exoplanets.
