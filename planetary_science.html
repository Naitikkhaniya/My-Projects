Planetary Science

Dive into the study of planets, moons, and other celestial bodies.

What is Planetary Science?

Planetary science is the study of planets, moons, and planetary systems, including their formation, composition, and dynamics. It is an interdisciplinary field involving geology, astronomy, and atmospheric sciences.

Key Topics in Planetary Science

  • Planetary Geology: Study of surface features and compositions.
  • Atmospheric Studies: Understanding the atmospheres of planets and moons.
  • Planetary Formation: Investigating how planets and moons are formed.
  • Habitability: Assessing conditions that support life.

Tools Used in Planetary Science

  • Space Probes: Gather data from distant planets.
  • Telescopes: Observe planetary systems.
  • Simulations: Model planetary processes and dynamics.

Why Study Planetary Science?

Understanding planetary systems helps us learn more about our own planet and the potential for life elsewhere in the universe.
